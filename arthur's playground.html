Bangkok is one of the most popular city to travel to in the world.
There are many popular destinations in this beautiful city.

    1. Wat Arun "Temple of dawn"

      Wat Arun, also known as the Temple of Dawn, is a stunning Buddhist temple located on the west bank of the Chao Phraya River in Bangkok, Thailand.
      It's known for its distinctive Khmer-style prang (tower) adorned with colorful porcelain and seashells.

    2.The Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew

      The Grand Palace is Bangkok’s most famous landmark, serving as the royal residence of Thai kings for over 150 years. Built in 1782 by King Rama I,
      it remains a symbol of Thailand’s rich history, culture, and craftsmanship.

    3.Lumphini Park

      Lumphini Park is a large public park in the heart of Bangkok.
      It offers a peaceful escape from the city and is a favorite spot for both locals and tourists.

    4.Yaowarat (Chinatown)

      Yaowarat is the largest Chinese community in Thailand.Yaowarat Road is known by tourists worldwide as a source of popular street food.
      There are restaurants both inside buildings and set up along the road.

    5.Siam Paragon

      Siam Paragon, Thailand's first largest world-class shopping center and entertainment complex, is remarked as a legendary must-visit destination for Thais and foreigners.
      The reputation is guaranteed by millions of visitors per year, who have traveled from every corner of the world.
